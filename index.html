Achievements

2015 Rensselaer Medalist

In the spring of my junior year, I was awarded the Rensselaer Medal from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, New York. The Rensselaer Medal is awarded to promising juniors who have distinguished themselves in mathematics and science. The award comes with a $15,000 per year scholarship for each medalist who applies, is accepted and chooses to enroll at Rensselaer.

National Honor Society

As a result of my number one class ranking, I was selected to be a part of the Weymouth High School Chapter of the National Honor Society, where I will seek a presidential nomination in the fall. My fellow peers and I are honored to be recognized in such a historic organization; moreover we look forward to the numerous charity work to be completed, including an annual blood drive in partnership with the Red Cross.

Bay State Conference All-Star, Football

In my junior season, I was named a first team all-conference selection for outstanding performance on both sides of the ball.

Weymouth High School Student-Athlete of the Month

In the fall of my junior year, October 2014, I was unanimously named the Weymouth High School Student-Athlete of the Month by coaches, teachers and administration across the district. This recognition provided me with a press opportunity in local media outlets to share my dual passion for studies and sports. I commended my fellow peers along the way for their support an effort; they are who truly make the game and the academic rigor so rewarding each and every day.

MIAA Sportsmanship Summit

In both my sophomore and junior year, I was selected among an elite group of student-athletes in Massachusetts to attend the annual Massachusetts Interscholastic Athletic Association Sportsmanship Summit. This eye-opening event, held at Gillette Stadium in Foxboro, Massachusetts by New England Patriots owner Robert Craft, offers a unique opportunity for student-athletes across the state to discuss areas for improvement not in terms of athletic talent, but rather integrity. I am able to draw from this invaluable experience to develop current leadership skills spanning the multiple organizations and programs where I currently occupy a guiding role.
